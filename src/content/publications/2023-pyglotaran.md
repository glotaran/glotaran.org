This paper describes the architecture and design of pyglotaran, demonstrating
its flexibility as an analysis framework through case studies including
photosynthesis energy transfer and photovoltaic systems.

The "LEGO-like" framing refers to the composable megacomplex system, which
allows kinetic models to be assembled from reusable building blocks rather
than reimplementing the full optimization for each new model type.
